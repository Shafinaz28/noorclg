General Nursing & Midwifery

GNM · Diploma

General Nursing
& Midwifery

The General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM) program at Noor College is a highly sought-after diploma course aimed at preparing students to become skilled nursing professionals with expertise in general nursing and midwifery care.

  • Comprehensive training in general nursing fundamentals
  • Clinical postings at partner hospitals across Bangalore
  • Midwifery and maternal care specialisation
  • Affiliated to RGUHS — nationally recognised qualification

B.Sc Nursing equips students with comprehensive medical knowledge and extensive practical training, preparing them for a rewarding, impactful career in modern healthcare environments across India and globally.

PB B.Sc Nursing · 2 Years

Post Basic
B.Sc Nursing

Designed for registered nurses seeking to enhance their qualifications and move into higher professional roles in healthcare. This 2-year program bridges practical nursing experience with advanced academic credentials.

  • Exclusively for registered nurses with GNM qualification
  • Accelerated pathway to bachelor-level credentials
  • Advanced clinical skills and leadership training
  • Opens pathway to M.Sc Nursing and beyond

Master of Science in Nursing

04
M.Sc Nursing · 2 Years

Master of Science
in Nursing

M.Sc Nursing enhances critical thinking and specialised care skills, preparing nurses for educator, administrator, and expert clinician roles. The program offers specialisation and advanced knowledge for leadership in nursing and research.

  • Specialisation tracks: Medical-Surgical, Community, Paediatric & more
  • Research methodology and evidence-based practice
  • Prepares for nurse educator and administrator roles
  • Pathway to doctoral studies and academic careers
