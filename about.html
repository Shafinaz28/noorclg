Who We Are

Noor College &
School of Nursing

Located in the vibrant city of Bangalore, Noor College & School of Nursing is a proud initiative of the Noor Education Trust. Established with a vision to deliver quality education in health sciences, the institution has emerged as a trusted name in nursing education across Karnataka.

We are affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS) and equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, modern laboratories, a digital library, and strong hospital partnerships that offer students real-time clinical exposure and hands-on learning.

At Noor, we integrate academic excellence with strong moral values, community responsibility, and innovative learning — because great nurses are built not just in classrooms, but through character.
