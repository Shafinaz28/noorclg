About the Programme

What is PB B.Sc Nursing?

The Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing (PB B.Sc. Nursing) is a two-year undergraduate programme designed for registered nurses (GNM diploma holders) who wish to upgrade their education and professional skills. Recognised by the Indian Nursing Council (INC), the programme builds on existing clinical experience and equips nurses with advanced knowledge, leadership abilities, and eligibility for higher studies.

This programme prepares graduates for senior clinical roles, teaching positions, administrative responsibilities, and global career opportunities — transforming working nurses into advanced nursing professionals.

Who Can Apply

Eligibility Criteria

This programme is designed exclusively for currently registered nurses. Here is what you need to qualify for admission at Noor College:

Educational Requirements

Diploma in General Nursing & Midwifery (GNM) from an INC-recognised institution
10+2 pass in any stream, or equivalent qualification recognised by the university
Must be a Registered Nurse and Registered Midwife (RN/RM) with the State Nursing Council

Additional Requirements

Valid RN/RM registration with the State Nursing Council of Karnataka
Certificate of medical fitness required at the time of admission
Reservation norms as per Government of Karnataka guidelines
Programme Goals

Programme Objectives

Graduates of the PB B.Sc. Nursing programme at Noor College will emerge as advanced nursing professionals equipped to lead, teach, and excel:

  • Upgrade professional competence as bedside nurses, nurse educators, and healthcare administrators.
  • Provide comprehensive nursing care using advanced clinical knowledge and evidence-based practices.
  • Take up leadership and supervisory roles in hospitals and healthcare organisations.
  • Promote community health awareness and evidence-based nursing practices in all settings.
  • Pursue higher education — M.Sc. Nursing, Nurse Practitioner programmes, or Ph.D.

The Upgrade Advantage

GNM vs PB B.Sc: What Changes?

Upgrading from GNM to PB B.Sc. Nursing is a career transformation — not just a qualification upgrade. Here's exactly what changes:

Before — GNM Graduate

Registered Nurse

  • Staff nurse roles only
  • Diploma-level position ceiling
  • Not eligible for M.Sc. direct entry
  • Restricted for teaching positions
  • Limited international eligibility
→Upgrade
After — PB B.Sc. Graduate

Advanced Nurse Professional

  • Senior clinical, educator & admin roles
  • Bachelor-level professional credibility
  • Eligible for M.Sc. Nursing directly
  • Qualified to teach in nursing institutions
  • Eligible for NCLEX, HAAD, DHA, MOH
