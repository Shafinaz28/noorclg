About Noor
Our Story

Compassion,
Competence & Character

Located at Bhoopasandra Main Road, Bangalore, Noor College & School of Nursing is a proud institution under the Noor Education Trust — committed to academic excellence and holistic development in the healthcare field.

We are affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS) and equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and hospital partnerships that prepare students for real-world nursing practice.

  • Affiliated to RGUHS – Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences
  • Modern labs, digital library & high-tech computer centre
  • Hospital partnerships for hands-on clinical training
  • Affordable fee structure with transport facilities
  • Strong focus on values, discipline & personal growth
