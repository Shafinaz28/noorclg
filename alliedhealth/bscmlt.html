B.Sc MLT

A 3-year Medical Laboratory Technology program focused on diagnostic testing, lab systems, and quality-driven clinical reporting.

Program Overview

B.Sc MLT trains students for diagnostic laboratory environments through practical learning in pathology, microbiology, hematology, and biochemistry operations.

  • Specimen collection, processing, and analysis workflows
  • Core laboratory disciplines and diagnostic methodologies
  • Quality assurance and laboratory safety practices
  • Hospital lab internships and practical reporting skills
