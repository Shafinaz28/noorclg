Occupational Therapy Program

Develop skills to help individuals recover independence in daily life through therapeutic interventions and activity-based rehabilitation.

Program Overview

Our Occupational Therapy track prepares students to assess functional challenges and deliver patient-centered therapy plans for physical, neurological, and developmental conditions.

  • Strong foundation in anatomy, psychology, and rehabilitation science
  • Training in activity-based and assistive therapeutic methods
  • Clinical exposure in hospitals and rehabilitation centers
  • Career pathways in rehab units, pediatrics, and community care
